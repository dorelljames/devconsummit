Offline Payments

1. Order the ticket from the ticket form above and click "Order Now"

2. Fill up the registration form and then under the "Purchase Tickets" (scroll to the bottom-most part of the registration page), click on "Show" under the "Other Payments Options"

3. Click "Pay By Check" and confirm your order

4. Deposit Cash/Check Payments to:

   Account Name: Ezra Jane Gayon
   Account Number: 4499-2850-81 (Peso)
   Bank: Bank of the Philippine Islands (BPI)
   Branch: BPI - eBloc 3, Cebu IT Park


5. Write legibly on your deposit slip or in the bank transfer screenshot the registered name/s and order #. If NO NAME & ORDER #, deposit slip/screenshot is considered INVALID.


6. Email scanned deposit slip/screenshot to cebu@devcon.ph with the details below. If you're a student, include a valid school ID.


  Subject: DevCon Summit 2018: Cebu Edition Payment -
  Message:
   1. Name/s of attendee with Order Number/s
   2. Category (Student/Professional)
   3. Date of Deposit
   4. Total amount deposited


7. Once you received a confirmation email from cebu@devcon.ph, your slot is already reserved.


IMPORTANT: Deadline for email submission is 5 business days after registration. Failure to comply automatically forfeits your registration.


NOTE: If you will request for an Acknowledgement Receipt, it will be issued during the DevCon Summit event.

1. For Paypal payment - you must submit a printed copy of the confirmation e-mail from Paypal.

2. For Bank deposit - you must submit a hard copy / photocopy of the deposit slip.
